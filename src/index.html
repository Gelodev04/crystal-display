Why TVs Fail in Window Displays

Consumer TVs are not designed for sustained daylight visibility. Content washes out and performance degrades under high ambient light.

Heat buildup leads to premature failure and inconsistent operation. Installations are improvised and vary site-to-site.

Central control is fragmented or manual, and responsibility is split across multiple vendors when something breaks.
